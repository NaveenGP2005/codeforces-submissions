A wall can consist of towers of different heights.</p><p>Horace was the first to finish making his wall. He called his wall an elephant. He can "see an elephant" on a segment of <span class="tex-span"><i>w</i></span> contiguous towers if the heights of the towers on the segment match as a sequence the heights of the towers in Horace's wall.
